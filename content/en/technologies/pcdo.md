## How it works

PCDO uses pulsed corona discharge in an **oxidative atmosphere** to generate ozone, hydroxyl radicals, and other reactive oxygen species. These powerful oxidants attack and break down organic molecules that resist conventional biological treatment.

## Key advantages

- Broad-spectrum effectiveness against refractory organics
- Full disinfection — inactivates bacteria, viruses, fungi, protozoa, and spores
- Removes colour, odour, and reduces TOC
- No chemical residuals — all oxidants decompose to oxygen and water
- Can be used standalone or as finishing stage after PCDR

## Target contaminants

- Refractory organic compounds (difficult-to-biodegrade)
- Pharmaceutical residues (antibiotics, hormones, cytostatics)
- Dyes and surfactants
- Pathogens — full spectrum sterilization
- VOCs and solvents
- Phenols and aromatic compounds
