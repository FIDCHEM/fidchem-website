## How it works

PCDR uses pulsed corona discharge in a **reducing atmosphere** to destroy halogenated organic compounds at the molecular level. The high-voltage nanosecond pulses generate reactive plasma species that break carbon-fluorine and carbon-chlorine bonds — the strongest bonds in organic chemistry, responsible for the persistence of PFAS/PFOS and dioxins.

## Key advantages

- Near-complete mineralization of PFAS/PFOS, including short-chain compounds resistant to other treatments
- Effective even in high-salinity and high-suspended-solids matrices (landfill leachate)
- No chemical additions required — plasma is generated from the atmosphere
- Scalable from laboratory to industrial flow rates
- Can be combined with PCDO for finishing treatment

## Target contaminants

PCDR is specifically designed for **halogenated persistent pollutants**:

- Per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS/PFOS) — including short-chain C4–C6
- Chlorinated and brominated organics
- Dioxins and furans (PCDD/PCDF)
- Chlorinated phenols and naphthalenes
