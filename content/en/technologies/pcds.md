## How it works

PCDS operates in a **nitrogen atmosphere**, generating reactive nitrogen species and UV radiation that inactivate all classes of microorganisms. Unlike PCDO, it does not oxidize organic compounds or alter the chemical composition of the water — making it ideal when water chemistry must remain unchanged.

## Key advantages

- Complete sterilization without chemical additions
- Water composition unchanged — no oxidation of TOC or minerals
- Inactivates even the most resistant plant viruses
- Suitable for pharmaceutical-grade water requirements
- Validated against bacteria, viruses, fungi, protozoa, and spores
