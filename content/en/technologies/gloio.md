## How it works

GLOIO filter blocks are manufactured from **microporous globular polymers** possessing both ion-exchange and chelating properties. The unique globular structure creates extremely high surface area with fast mass transfer kinetics — allowing rapid removal of trace contaminants even at very low concentrations.

## Key advantages

- Ultra-fast adsorption kinetics — short contact times
- Effective at very low contaminant concentrations (ppb range)
- Available as disposable cartridges (up to 1 year lifespan) or regenerable blocks
- No secondary waste from chemical regeneration when used in regenerable mode
- Works effectively in high-salinity matrices

## Target contaminants

- PFAS/PFOS — including short-chain compounds
- Heavy metals (lead, cadmium, mercury, copper, zinc)
- Iron(III) and manganese
- Chromium(VI) and arsenite/arsenate
- Anionic and cationic dyes
- Surfactants
- Nitrates
- Colour and turbidity
